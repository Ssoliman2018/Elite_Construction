About Company

Elite Company Steel (ECS)

is a global company of independent companies conducting turnkey engineering projects, installing production lines, steel decoration, steel construction work and steel structures fabrication.

We are also a supplier of steel materials. We work with clients in various industries to design, installing, supply and maintain projects.
